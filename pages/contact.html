Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in a paint party package?

Each package includes all necessary art supplies (canvas, paints, brushes, easels, aprons), professional instruction, setup and cleanup. We bring everything needed for a successful creative session!

Do I need any painting experience?

Not at all! Our classes are designed for all skill levels, from complete beginners to experienced painters. Our instructors provide step-by-step guidance throughout the session.

How long does a typical paint party last?

Most sessions run for 2-3 hours, but we can customize the duration based on your event needs and the complexity of the chosen painting.

What's the minimum/maximum group size?

We accommodate groups from 6 to 30 people. For larger groups, please contact us to discuss special arrangements.

Can we choose our own painting design?

Yes! We have a gallery of popular designs to choose from, or we can work with you to create a custom design that fits your event theme.

Do you travel to different locations?

Yes, we serve Dickinson and surrounding areas. We can set up at various venues including homes, businesses, community centers, breweries, and more!
